Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my progress across devices?

Your progress automatically syncs when you're connected to the internet. The game uses anonymous authentication, so your scores and unlocks are tied to your device's unique ID. To transfer progress to a new device, use the backup code from your profile settings.

What are the different game modes?

Classic: Traditional gameplay at normal speed
Speed Run: Faster gameplay with double score multiplier
Endurance: Slower speed for longer games
Multi-Plane: Focus on exploring different planes with 3x multiplier

How do I unlock new truck skins and trails?

Free cosmetics are unlocked by reaching certain rank milestones. Premium cosmetics can be purchased from the in-game store. All cosmetics are purely visual and don't affect gameplay.

My game is crashing or not loading properly

Try these steps:
1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
2. Update to the latest version of the game
3. Clear the app cache (on mobile)
4. Restart your device
If issues persist, contact us with your device model and OS version.

First, try restarting the game. If items still don't appear, tap "Restore Purchases" in the store. Keep your receipt from Apple/Google and contact us if the issue persists.

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues

If you're having trouble connecting to leaderboards:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure the game has permission to access the internet
  • Try switching between WiFi and mobile data
  • Check if your firewall or VPN is blocking the connection

Performance Issues

For the best experience:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Ensure your device has at least 1GB of free storage
  • Update to the latest OS version supported by your device
  • Try lowering graphics settings if available
