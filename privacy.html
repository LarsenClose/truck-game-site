Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 28, 2025

Larsen Close ("we," "our," or "us") operates Truck: Dig & Drive (the "Game"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Game and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

Our Game uses anonymous authentication and does not collect personally identifiable information. We collect:

  • Anonymous user ID (generated by Firebase)
  • Generated username (e.g., "FastTrucker123")
  • Game scores and statistics
  • Gameplay timestamps

Game Data

When you play and submit scores to the global leaderboard, we collect:

  • Game scores and performance metrics
  • Game mode played (Classic, Endurance, Time Attack)
  • Truck length and planes explored
  • Game duration
  • Timestamp of score submission
  • Total games played and personal best scores

Use of Data

Truck: Dig & Drive uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Game
  • To provide global leaderboards and competitive features
  • To notify you about changes to our Game
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our Game
  • To monitor the usage of our Game
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase services for:

  • Firebase Authentication: Anonymous authentication only - no email or personal info required
  • Cloud Firestore: Store game scores and leaderboard data

Firebase may collect certain information automatically, including device information and usage statistics. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Game is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The game uses anonymous authentication and does not require personal information to play.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain data protection rights:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data through the in-app profile settings
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: support@larsenclose.com
